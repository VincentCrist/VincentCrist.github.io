VR In Golf
Site Author
 Phil


Abstract
 Virtual reality in golf is not a new invention, it has been around since the 80s. This article goes over the benefits of VR in golf, comparing VR golf to real golf and the challenges of using this technology. The benefits of this technology is that it gives golfers a practice environment that helps them better swing paths and swing mechanics. After they play in VR they can look over their swing and change minor details about it and see how it helps them. Some challenges of this technology is needing a headset and to the average golfer and highschool golfer this can be a challenge to purchase one. To the upper college and pro level this is a good idea because it helps them play without playing in the sun, being outside and they can be in the comfort of their own home.

Abstract
 In 2017 the NBA offered VR courtside. They offered this because some fans want to watch their favorite team and players play but some of the tickets could cost up to $1,400. This article goes into detail about how the meta quest 3 will show courtside VR and how this technology compares to in person games. The Meta Quest 3 is a virtual reality headset that you can buy that has lots of different games on it and one of them include the Xadium app that you can download and watch most of the NBA regular season games courtside using this headset.

Video Replay
Site Author
 MLB


Abstract
 This article by the MLB goes over the rule of video replay and what it exactly does and is. Video replay is if a call is made in a game by the umpire and the player or manager does not agree with the call, they have the option to challenge the play meaning the umpires in the replay command center, in New York, look over the play and determine if the call stands or if the call will be overturned. The only way the call can be overturned is if there is 100 percent proof that the original call was wrong.

 Trackman
Site Author
 Brent Pourciau
Abstract
 This article tells us how trackman is used in baseball. Trackman is used in baseball by helping pitchers improve their pitches from using data analytics. It uses a doppler radar device to track the baseball right out of the pitcher's hand to the catcher, and while this is happening it gathers information like spin rate, spin axis, release point and pitch velocity. All of this info is then looked over by players and coaches to help the pitcher get better. Installing a trackman only takes 4 steps and will be working in about 10 minutes. There is a downside to having one of these. Brand new these things can cost upwards of $20,000.
